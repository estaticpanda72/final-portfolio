Eye Tracking Process

Background


Hello there! Let's talk a bit about the idea behind the eyetracking experience. This was the first project I tackled as part of a group of designers. It all began with an initial bakery webpage design. Using this as a starting point, my group split into two groups to create two versions. This involved adding photos, changing the size, typeface, and layout of text, drastically changing the color scheme, adding content, etc. My group decided to make one version pretty wild and eyecatching with mismatched fonts, bright colors, and unaligned images; the other version would the polar opposite. This was done with the intent of being able to observe how much eye tracking data would differ across the two versions.
